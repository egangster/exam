The Galaxy

The galaxy is a very large place. While we live in the Milky Way galaxy there are estimated
to be 100 billion other ones. It is said our galaxy has 100-400 billion stars. They estimate that the diameter is
100,000-180,000 light years.

Planets

Mercury

The planet Mercury has zero moons. Along with your weight on Mercury being 38% less than what it is on earth.
A year on Mercury is 88 Earth days.

Venus

The planet Venus is the hottest in the solar system because it has a thick atmosphere.
Like Mercury, Venus also has zero moons.

Earth

The planet Earth is the only planet capable of humans living on it.
Earth's year is 365 Earth days long.

Mars

A year on the planet Mars is 687 Earth days long.
There have been 39 missions to Mars, only 16 have been successful.

Jupiter

The planet Jupiter has the shortest day of all planets.
The planet's year is 12 Earth years.

Saturn

The planet Saturn has 62 moons.
A year on Saturn is 29 Earth years.

Uranus

The planet Uranus hits the coldest temperatures out of all the planets.
A year on Uranus is 84 Earth years.

Neptune

The planet Neptune has the second largest gravity. (Largest is Jupiter)
A year on Neptune is 165 Earth Years.
